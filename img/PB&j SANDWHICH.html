PB&J sandwhich

This is a recipe on how to make a PB&J sandwhich.

A PB&J sandwhich is a very simple but delicious recipe. Many people have made a PB&J sandwhich.

Ingrediants

  • -Any type of jelly (preferably grape jelly)
  • -2 slices of bread
  • -fresh peannut butter

Instructions

  1. Lay 2 pieces of bread on a plate
  2. Spread peanut buttter on 1 slice
  3. Spread jelly on the other slice of bread
  4. attach the two pieces of bread together and enjoy!
