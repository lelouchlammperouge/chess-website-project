How the pieces move.


The pawn moves 2 squres form its begining position but can also move 1 square
after it moves once it cant move twice again. A pawn only kills diagonally 1 square.
a special function of the pawn is that it can be promoted to another piece when it reaches
the other end of the board.

The rook moves vertically and horizontaly exclusively. It can move through the
entire board.

The bishop moves diagonally across the entire board.

The knight moves in an L and can jump over pieces which makes it very unique.

the queen can wherever she wants diagonaly or straight.

the king can move 1 square wherever but it cant move in quaes whrere it is in
danger. The king is the most important piece yet the weakest pieces. When you king gets
captured you loose the game.
